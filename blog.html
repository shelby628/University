Our Certificate and Online Programmes for 2025

Maseno University offers a diverse range of certificate and online programmes for 2025 through its innovative eCampus platform, catering to learners seeking flexible and accessible education. The certificate programmes span various disciplines, including Arts and Social Sciences, Business and Economics, Education, Development and Strategic Studies, Computing and Informatics, and Physical and Biological Sciences. Notable offerings include certificates in Journalism & Mass Media, Business Administration, Special Needs Education, Public Administration, Information & Communication Technology, and Environmental Impact Assessment, among others


The eCampus facilitates online delivery of high-quality courses, allowing students to learn at their own pace and convenience. This mode of study is particularly beneficial for individuals balancing work, family, or residing in remote areas. The programmes are structured to provide reliable access to course materials, eLibrary resources, and administrative support anytime, anywhere .


For the 2025 academic year, Maseno University has intakes in January, May, and September, though not all programmes admit students in every intake. Prospective students are required to pay a non-refundable application fee of Ksh. 2,000 and can submit their applications either physically or, for international applicants, via email . The eCampus's flexible modular approach allows learners to pay for the number of modules they wish to take, making education more affordable and tailored to individual needs .
