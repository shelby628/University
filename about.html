About Us

We are the only university located along the equator

Maseno University is a public institution of higher learning located in Maseno, Kisumu County, Kenya. Established as a full-fledged university in 2001, it originated from the merger of Maseno Government Training Institute and Siriba Teacher’s Training College in 1990, initially forming Maseno University College under Moi University

At Maseno University, our graduates stand out in the global job market. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, practical skills, and industry-relevant training, our programs are designed to prepare students for real-world success. Employers across Kenya and around the world recognize and value the quality of our degrees, making our alumni highly competitive and employable. Choosing Maseno University is not just a step toward earning a degree — it is a sure path to a promising and rewarding career.
